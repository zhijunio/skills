# English Tells

Apply when `language_lane: en`. Cross-check `patterns.md` classes.

## AI vocabulary cluster (cap per paragraph)

Additionally, crucial, delve, landscape (abstract), pivotal, showcase, underscore, vibrant, tapestry, testament, fostering, garner, intricate, interplay, align with, highlight (verb overuse)

→ Replace with plain verbs: use, show, is, has, help, change

## Construction tells

| Tell | Prefer |
|------|--------|
| serves as / stands as / marks a | is / are |
| It's not just X, it's Y | One sentence on Y |
| From X to Y, from A to B (false range) | List with commas |
| Not only… but also… (paired) | Single claim |
| Despite challenges… continues to thrive | Specific obstacle + fact |
| In order to / Due to the fact that | to / because |
| could potentially / might arguably | may / (delete) |

## Chatbot artifacts (delete)

- Great question! / Certainly! / You're absolutely right!
- Here is an overview… / I hope this helps / Let me know if…
- As of my last update / While details are limited…

## Style

- Em dashes — max one per paragraph; prefer commas or split
- Title Case In Every Heading → sentence case
- Emoji-prefixed bullets → plain bullets or prose
- Bold **Term:** list items → flowing sentence

## Rhythm

- Alternate short punch and longer explanatory sentences
- First person OK when draft already informal; don't inject fake anecdotes
